No Wifi Games

No Wifi Games is an offline mini puzzle game collection for iOS. This page describes what the app does for players.


GAMES

The app ships with 11 handcrafted classic casual games, all available offline in a single download.

Number & Logic Puzzles
• 2048 — Slide and merge numbers to reach the 2048 tile and beyond.
• 1010! — Place block pieces on a grid and clear full rows or columns.
• Sudoku — Classic 9×9 sudoku with three difficulty levels.
• Minesweeper — The timeless mine-finder with three difficulty levels.

Strategy & Planning
• Tetres — A Tetris-style falling-block game with line clears and increasing speed.
• Water Sort — Liquid sorting puzzle across three difficulty levels.
• Merge Puzzle — A flood-fill style merge game with chain reactions.

Action & Reflex
• Clumsy Bird — Tap to fly a bird through pipe gaps.
• Cat Trap — Surround and trap the wandering cat on a hex board (10 levels).

Puzzle & Exploration
• Word Search — Find hidden words in a letter grid.
• Cat & Mouse — A maze strategy game where you guide the mouse to the cheese.


FEATURES

• All-in-one library — 11 classic games in one app. No need to download multiple apps.
• 100% offline — No internet connection required. Play on subways, flights, or anywhere with no signal.
• No ads, no in-app purchases — One download, forever free. No pop-ups, no upsells.
• No login — Open and play instantly. No account, no registration.
• Daily featured game — A different game is highlighted on the home screen each day to help you explore the whole collection.
• Graduation system — Each game has a long-term Graduation Goal. Reach it to earn the Master title, with a gold progress bar that is saved permanently.
• Per-game high scores — Best scores are tracked independently for each game and difficulty.
• Mid-game saves — Select titles support resume from where you left off.
• Privacy first — Zero data collection, zero network requests, zero tracking, zero sensitive permissions.
• Age rating 4+ — Suitable for all ages.
