Privacy Policy

ThoughtTrail is an AI-powered reflection companion. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may process, how AI features work, and what choices you have.

1. Information you provide

When you use ThoughtTrail, you may enter concerns, answers, notes, and other text for reflection. The app may also generate summaries, follow-up questions, and suggested next steps based on your input.

Please do not enter sensitive personal information such as passwords, payment details, government identification numbers, precise addresses, medical records, or private information about other people.

2. AI processing

ThoughtTrail uses artificial intelligence to generate questions and summaries. To provide these features, your input and the related conversation context may be sent to an AI service provider for processing and response generation.

AI-generated content may be inaccurate, incomplete, or biased. It is provided for personal reflection and organization only. It is not medical, legal, financial, psychological, therapeutic, or other professional advice.

3. Local history

If history features are enabled in the app, completed reflections and summaries may be stored locally on your device so you can review them later. You can delete saved history items in the app. Deleting the app may also remove locally stored app data, subject to your device and backup settings.

4. API settings and tokens

The app may allow API configuration or may use a configured AI provider to deliver AI functionality. API tokens, when stored by the app, are intended to be kept securely on the device where possible. Do not share your API token with others.

5. Diagnostics and app operations

Depending on your device settings and App Store distribution settings, Apple or the app environment may provide crash logs, diagnostics, and performance information. This information is used to understand reliability issues and improve the app experience.

6. Third-party services

AI requests may be processed by third-party AI infrastructure. These providers process transmitted content to generate responses and operate the service. Their handling of data may be governed by their own terms and privacy policies. We recommend avoiding sensitive information in prompts because AI processing can involve external systems.

7. Children

ThoughtTrail is not designed for children to submit personal information. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the issue.

8. Your choices

  • You can choose what text to enter into the app.
  • You can avoid entering sensitive or identifying information.
  • You can delete local history items in the app where available.
  • You can contact us with privacy questions or deletion requests related to information we may control.

9. International use

If you use ThoughtTrail outside your country or region, your information may be processed in locations where our service providers operate. Data protection rules may differ from those in your location.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will revise the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or ThoughtTrail, contact us at gamefan9688@qq.com.
