About EmuCool

Our Story

EmuCool was born from the hands of a group of developers who love retro games. We miss those wonderful times playing games with friends, and the pure joy that classic games brought us.

In this fast-paced era, we hope to create a warm nostalgic space for players through EmuCool, allowing everyone to find their own gaming sanctuary in the busy life.

From a small initial project to a mature emulator today, EmuCool has always been user experience-centered, continuously optimizing and improving to provide players with the purest retro gaming experience.

EmuCool Pro

EmuCool offers an optional auto-renewing subscription, EmuCool Pro, with monthly and yearly plans. Pro benefits include:

  • Premium display filters (CRT, TV, LCD) for a retro look
  • 10 save slots per game (instead of 4)
  • Ad-free experience

Payment is charged to your Apple ID account; subscription renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel in Settings → [your Apple ID] → Subscriptions.

Important Notice

EmuCool does not include any game ROMs or copyrighted content. The game screens and names shown in preview images are for demonstration only. You must provide your own legally obtained game files (ROMs) to use the full functionality of this app.

Please ensure you have the legal right to use the games you import and comply with local copyright laws. EmuCool assumes no responsibility for any game content provided by users.
